About NyaySetu

NyaySetu is a legal-technology platform designed to simplify access to professional legal consultation through a secure and structured WhatsApp-based ecosystem.

The platform integrates an intelligent WhatsApp Legal Chatbot that provides preliminary legal information and guided consultation scheduling before connecting users with experienced legal professionals.
