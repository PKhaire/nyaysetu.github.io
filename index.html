About NyaySetu

NyaySetu is a structured legal-technology platform designed to simplify access to professional legal consultation through a secure and compliant WhatsApp-based ecosystem.

The platform integrates an intelligent Legal Chatbot that provides preliminary legal process guidance before facilitating consultation scheduling with independent legal professionals.

NyaySetu ensures structured workflows, confidential communication standards, and secure digital payment processing powered by Razorpay.
