Refund Policy

NyaySetu operates on a scheduled consultation booking model.

Refunds will be considered only if NyaySetu fails to deliver the confirmed consultation at the exact date and time selected by the user during booking.

Refunds are not applicable in the following cases:

  • User fails to attend the scheduled consultation (no-show).
  • User cancels the booking after confirmation.
  • Incorrect details provided by the user at the time of booking.

Eligible refund requests must be sent to outsidethecourt@gmail.com within 24 hours from the scheduled consultation time.
