Data Deletion Policy

NyaySetu respects user privacy and provides users with the ability to request deletion of their personal information shared through our website, WhatsApp-based consultation system, or related services.

How to Request Data Deletion

Users who wish to request deletion of their personal data may submit a written request to:

legalhelp@nyaysetu.in

The subject line should include: "Data Deletion Request"

Verification Process

For security and confidentiality purposes, NyaySetu may verify the identity of the requesting individual before processing any deletion request.

Processing Timeline

Upon successful verification, relevant personal data will be deleted within 7 working days, unless retention is required for compliance with applicable legal or regulatory obligations.

Scope of Deletion

  • Personal contact information provided during booking
  • Consultation-related data shared voluntarily
  • Associated communication records

Once deleted, data cannot be restored.

For urgent concerns, users may also contact us via call at +91 7020030080.
